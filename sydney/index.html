Workshops

Schedule

The ResBaz program runs for 3 full days. The first 2 days will focus on learning new skills through hands-on workshops, with a number of different streams on offer. The third day, Festival Day, will provide a chance to relax and get to know people you've been learning with. Festivities will include lightning talks, networking opportunities, industry talks, tech stalls and much more.

Registrations will open in May. If you would like to be notified as more information becomes available, please subscribe to our mailing list below. If you have specific questions send us an email via the link at the bottom.
