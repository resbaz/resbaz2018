10 Eco Data Things


10 Eco Data Things is an interactive workshop in which we will explore issues surrounding management of research data, specifically for people working with ecological data. This workshop was developed from the 23 (research data) Things program and the extensive ANDS resources and materials related to research data management and reuse.

The workshop will include activities that will help you to increase your data skills such as data discovery, data sharing, licensing and citation, and data management plans. We will give you an overview of practical, free, online resources and tools that you can begin using today to enhance your understanding and use of ecological research data.

This workshop is based of 23 Research Data Things by ANDS: https://www.ands.org.au/working-with-data/skills/23-research-data-things
